About Me

My name is Lorraine Ly and I am currently a student majoring in studio art with a digital art concentration at Brooklyn College. I have enjoyed drawing and arts and crafts as a child and by taking various subjects throughout high school and college, it became certain to me that I would want to pursue art for my future education and career. I enjoy creating art using different kinds of mediums as a form of expression. Here in my website, I would like to showcase my artwork and style through a diverse range of mediums. I mainly explore and work with color but also utilize symbolism and surrealism in some of my artworks as well.
